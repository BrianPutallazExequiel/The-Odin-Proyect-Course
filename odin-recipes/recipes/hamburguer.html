Hamburger

Description:

A hamburger is a classic American comfort food. It consists of a seasoned ground beef patty, typically cooked on a grill or stovetop, served inside a soft bun with various toppings and condiments. It's a versatile dish that can be customized to suit your taste, making it a favorite for many.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of ground beef (80% lean)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 4 slices of cheese (e.g., cheddar, American, Swiss)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Pickle slices
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise (optional)

Steps:

  1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into burger patties. Season both sides with salt and black pepper.
  2. Preheat a grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat. Cook the burger patties for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare or longer to your desired level of doneness.
  3. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty and cover the grill or pan to melt the cheese.
  4. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster until lightly browned.
  5. Assemble your hamburgers: Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom bun, followed by the burger patty with melted cheese, sliced tomatoes, onions, and pickle slices.
  6. Add condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise if desired.
  7. Top with the other half of the bun, and your delicious homemade hamburger is ready to enjoy!
